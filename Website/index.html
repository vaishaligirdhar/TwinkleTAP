About TwinkleTAP

TwinkleTAP is a fun and exciting VR piano game aimed at enhancing players' soft skills in a fun and exciting manner. The game is developed for Oculus and LEAP motion so the user can plan the game using their hand gestures.

What is Gamification
of Education?

Gamification of education is the approach of using game techniques in a learning environment to motivate students to learn.
This game is designed such that users may also use it in their own personal time.

Why Music?

English skills

“Music skills were found to correlate significantly with both phonological awareness and reading development in 4- and 5-year-old children”
McMaster University, Canada

Math skills

“Musical elements such as steady beat, rhythm, melody, and tempo possess inherent mathematical principles such as spatial properties, sequencing, counting, patterning, and one-to-one correspondence”
National Association for the Education of Young Children, USA

Interpersonal/Intrapersonal skills

“Enjoyable and rewarding engagement with music has a positive effect on the social and personal development of children and young people”
University of London, UK

Vaishali Girdhar

Creator, TwinkleTAP

“This game is designed to allow players to learn soft skills such as development of audio-visual and motor skills including areas such as peripheral vision and hand-eye coordination. The game will provide a fun way to allow players to develop these skills on their own.”
